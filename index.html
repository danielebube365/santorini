Our Story
A Greek space,
a world of food.

Tucked into Maitama, Santorini brings a little of the Greek islands to Abuja: whitewashed walls, soft blue light, and a space made for slowing down. From morning brunch to a late-night cocktail, the doors stay open and the vibe stays easy.

The look is Greek, but the kitchen travels the world. Pasta, grills, sandwiches, sharing platters and plates from everywhere, all served in one of Abuja's most beautiful rooms.
